Shahe Culture & Sports Centre Badminton Gym 沙河文体羽毛球馆

Address: Next to Tianhe Garden, Xinzhong Road, Shahe East Road 地址：沙河东路拐新中路，靠近天河公园

Transportation: From Keyuan Metro Station to Window of the World, and change to Green Line, then go to Exit B of Baishizhou Metro Station. Walk along Shahe East Road, and turn right to reach Xinzhong Road, walk for a few meters, and the entrance is on your left hand side, about 11mins on foot. 交通：从科苑地铁站坐到世界之窗，转罗宝线，抵达白石洲地铁B出口。延沙河东路走，右拐至新中路，入口在走几米后的你的左手边，走路约11分钟。
